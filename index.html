LLAMAS LOVE TO SPIT

Llamas can regurgitate their stomach contents and spit up to ten feet! They often spit at other "lower ranking" llamas as a form of discipline. Sometimes they even spit at humans! Here's a compilation of people getting spit on by llamas. Neat!

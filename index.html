LLAMAS LOVE TO SPIT

Llamas can regurgitate their stomach contents and spit up to ten feet! They often spit at other "lower ranking" llamas as a form of discipline. Sometimes they even spit at humans! Here's a compilation of people getting spit on by llamas. Neat!

LLAMAS ARE RELATED TO CAMELS

Although they don't have humps, llamas can survive on very little water. This trait has made them the ideal travelling companion for humans since 100 AD! Llamas are indigenous to South America, and are well built to handle the mountainous terrain.
